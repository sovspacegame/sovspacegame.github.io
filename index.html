Rules and game features


Disclaimer: playing is on your own risk! Please keep in mind, a game-smartcontract is a complex system and can contain several attack-vectors and bugs. Please do only play with token you are willing to lose.

This game based on team-work, common tactics and pure destruction - no land-sale, no new tokens or NFT-drops.


The basic game rules (please read carefully):
  • The aim of the game is to destroys as many as possible structures of the opposing species.
  • At first the player must choose a species (currently humans or greys). This decision cannot be revoked.
  • Structures are minted as NFT with an amount of SOV. This Structures can be permanently placed (build) on an area.
  • 10% of the SOV will become burned during the minting. The remaining 90% will back the structure.
  • If a structure becomes destroyed by the opposing species, the backed SOV will be send to the winner, the NFT will become burned.
  • The lander can be placed on every undeveloped area of the surface.
  • All structures (execpt the lander) must be build next on existing structure or street of your own species.
  • If you build next to an existing building, please ask the owner for permission. He has the right to demolish younger adjoining structures.
  • A demolish-action will result in a 100% burning of the backed SOV.
  • The only mobile element is the bulldozer. Please switch from scroll to move - mode in order to move the mobile element.
  • Bulldozers can freely move and attack opponent structures.
  • The player who builds/placed a structure can set a personal nickname, storend in the mutual NFT-attributes.

Other general comments:
  • This game runs on EOS mainnet and is using the SOV-token.
  • The gameplay will become extended in time - more features, weapons and defense-elements. This development depends from the general user-feedback.
  • The game-frontend is only using public API-nodes. All data are loaded in realtime from blockchain (EOS mainnet).
  • Because of the limitations of the API, you should only use the reload-action, if needed.
  • You can download the game-frontend from Github and play on your own local device.
  • For bug-reporting please ask in SOV-Telegram channel: https://t.me/eossov
  • Play fair, well-considered but without any mercy


The Lander

This is the only structure you can be placed on every free area of the surface. All other structures must be build next to an existing structure from your species. A lander cannot be placed on an existing street, only on natural surfaces.

The Residence

Your people should live somewhere. Requires an existing building or street.

Agriculture

Your people need food from time totime. Requires a residence next to.

Factory

At some point, you like to produce something, like a bulldozer. Requires an Agriculture next to.

Bulldozer

The first mobile unit in the game. A bulldozer can move and attack opponent structures. Requires an factory next to. A bulldozer can destroy oppenent structures and the owner will get the backed SOV-token after successful destruction. If a bulldozer is attacked by another bulldozer, it will attack back in the same moment. So keep in mind, if you attack with another bulldozer, it will likely result in your own destruction.

Street

A street is an inexpensive structure where you can build other structures next to. You cannot destroy a street with a bulldozer, but you can build a street from your own species over it. The SOV costs will become burned during the minting process and cannot be refunded. If you drive with a mobile object (like a bulldozer) on a street, the costs will be refunded. So driving on a street is saving many fuel.

Wall

A wall is a strong element and cannot become destroyed by a bulldozer (type I). Please use walls in order to protect your important buildings.

Collector

With a Collector facility you can earn SVX-token. On every bulldozer move some SOV will be used for SVX-mining. Every 24 hours a collector will be able to claim a share from this pool. The more active the players are, the more SVX are generated.

Tower

A tower is a building for defense. If an enemy vehicle moves through the view of a tower (red area on click), it suffers damage. As a ranged weapon, if a vehicle is destroyed, the SOV for SVX mining is burned and will not be credited to the owner of the defence-tower.
